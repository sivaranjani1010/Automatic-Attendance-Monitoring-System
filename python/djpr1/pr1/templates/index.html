About the Department

The Department of Information Technology at CIET (established in 2001) provides top-class academic training, modern IT labs, and industry collaborations. We nurture innovators, leaders, and skilled IT professionals ready to solve global challenges.

Mission
  • Provide strong technical foundations in IT.
  • Encourage innovation, research, and projects.
  • Produce socially responsible technocrats.
Vision

To be a global leader in IT education, research, and entrepreneurship, fostering innovation with ethical values.
